How to Play

Goal: Keep your village healthy and hydrated!

Health: Your village needs supplies. Health decreases over time, especially if water is low.

Water: Essential resource that keeps the village healthy. Water decreases every 3 seconds.

Money: Use money to buy buildings. Each building provides health and water benefits.

How to build: Click on a tile to select it, then choose a building from the shop.

Game Over: When health reaches 0, the game ends!
